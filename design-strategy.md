# Design Strategy

## Brand Voice
- Simple, clear, and direct.
- Friendly, professional, and helpful.
- Avoid corporate jargon or buzzwords.

## Style Guide
- **Primary Color:** `#0066cc` (Deep Blue)
- **Secondary Color:** `#f4f4f9` (Light Slate Gray)
- **Font Family:** 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Spacing Grid:** 4px grid (spacing should be in multiples of 4px: 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px, etc.)
